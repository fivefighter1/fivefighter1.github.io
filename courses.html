  • How do I select a session?
      1. Open your transcript (Learning tab)
      2. Locate the training title
      3. Click the 'Select Session' hyperlink
      4. Choose a session from those listed

      Additional Info
  • How do I withdraw from a session?
      1. Open up your Transcript (Learning tab)
      2. Locate the Session in your list of active training
      3. Click the 'Withdraw' hyperlink
      4. On the resulting screen choose an option from the drop down
      5. Briefly describe the reason for withdrawal in the text box
  • How do I know if a WebEx is available?
      1. Search for the ILT course (event/session)
      2. Click the hyperlinked title of the course
      3. WebEx availability will be listed in the course description

      Additional Info
  • Where can I find the room location for my class?
      1. Open your transcript (Learning tab)
      2. Click on the hyperlinked title of the class
      3. The room name will be listed in the 'Special Instructions' section

      The room location is also listed in the email invitation sent just after registration (check your Outlook Calendar)

  • What if I don’t know the course name or keywords?
      On the Home Page, in Browse for Training box:

      1. Click plus sign next to topic names to see subcategories
      2. Click subcategory (e.g. Training by Business Line includes ATC, Professional Services, Sales, and Supply Chain and Logistics subcategories)
      3. Click any one of these to display its courses
      4. Use the filters to the left to narrow results
